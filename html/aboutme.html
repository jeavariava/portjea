About Me

Hi! I'm Jea, a sophomore communication design student. My interests lie in graphics and illustration and I explore a lot of different art styles through my work. Most of my art tends to lean towards the softer side, with pastels colours and shades of pink making frequent appearances. Music is my biggest design inspiration, my favourite song artists being BTS and Megan Thee Stallion! I hope you enjoy going through my work and make sure to let me know what you think.
